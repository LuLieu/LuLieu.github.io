VOICE Project Overview

Valuing Our Integrity, Consent, and Empowerment

1. Project Overview

  • Purpose: To promote awareness and education for survivors of sexual assault through a safe, informative, and interactive club website.
  • Intended Users: Survivors, allies, campus community members seeking support and education.
  • Content: Resources, event calendar, anonymous story submission, survivor stories, about us.

5. Pages

  • Home - Welcomes visitor, explains use and navigation
  • Resources - New page, shows local resources for CLT students and explains signs of distress.
  • Event Calendar - jQuery Calendar, showing month, year, days, and events on those days.
  • Anonymous Form - Non-saving form that confirms submission with a message.
  • Survivor Stories - Displays survivor stories with a carousel of images
  • About Us - Explains the founder, Amy, and her drive to make this club.

6. Dynamic Functionality

  • Anonymous Form: AJAX form submission with confirmation message
  • Event Calendar: Interactive jQuery calendar widget
  • Survivor Stories Gallery: jQuery model or carousel for story previews/fullscreen
