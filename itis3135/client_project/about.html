Our Story

VOICE was founded by Amy, a passionate advocate for survivors of sexual assault. After experiencing firsthand how isolating recovery can feel, Amy created VOICE to offer a safe and empowering space to discover resources and find the help she needed.

Her mission is to promote education, healing, and action - making sure no one has to navigate their experience alone.
