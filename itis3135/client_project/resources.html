Signs Someone Might Be in Distress

  • Sudden withdrawal from friends or activities
  • Unexplained mood swings or irritability
  • Changes in appearance or sleep habits
  • Fearful or anxious behavior, especially around certain people
  • Reluctance to talk about certain topics
