Why We're Here

It's an unfortunate truth that many people are unsafe in their current or past relationships. When our founder sought out community after complications with her past relationships that affected her every day life and well-being, she decided to create a safe space that allows all students to learn, heal, and spread awareness on the signs of sexual assault in your own or others' relationships.

At VOICE, we aim to offer a sense of community and resources to those in need. Find out how you can help today!
