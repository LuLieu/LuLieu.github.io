What is Competitive Marvel Rivals?

Competitive Marvel Rivals is a subcategory of Marvel Rivals players who compete against each other for money ($14,500), in-game items, or just bragging rights. The competitive scene is popular among collegiates as universities often brawl against each other for prizes.

Who is this for?

If you, like many, are seeking refuge from the horrors that is Overwatch 2, Marvel Rivals is the perfect video game for you. It combines all of your favorite MCU characters into a fun team-based hero combat game. However, many choose to take their gaming experience to the next level by entering competitive gamemodes and fighting to be the best.

How do the seasons work?

About every 2 months, the developers of Marvel Rivals will push an update that contains a multitude of changes and additions to maintain gamer interests. With these updates, all player's ranks will be reduced by a constant amount and they will have to rebuild their ranks over the course of the new season.

Where do these events take place?

Since the pandemic, gaming tournaments have taken a turn toward being online rather than a local area network (LAN). This would mean that players could play from the comfort of their own home offices to compete in competitive tournaments. Before the pandemic (and once again rising in popularity), competitive LAN environments were the arena of choice.

Why does competitiveness matter?

People are driven to competitive environments for many reasons. It could be because of the $14,500 prize pool, or as simple as the Invisible Woman recolor skin. I personally like the adrenaline rush of getting into good fights and being rewarded for exceptional teamwork and communication.

Those who play for their college e-Sports teams may also be eligible for benefits. Players may be recognized by official gaming teams and associations, allowing them to have access to paid contracts, free housing, and sometimes, tuition reimbursement. Otherwise, scholarships are popular outcomes of collegiate teams excelling in their field, and this is no different for Marvel Rivals.
